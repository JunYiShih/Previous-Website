About Me

I am a graduate student of Harbin Institue of Technology (HIT). At present, I am working with Pro.Fusheng Zha and his research group at State Key Laboratory of Robotics and System.

My research topics include Visual SLAM, 3D Reconstruction and State Estimation for Mobile Robots. I sincerely hope that my research work can improve the lives of ordinary people, even if only a little bit.
